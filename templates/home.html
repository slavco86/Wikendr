What is Wikendr?

Have you ever had a feeling, when you wake up on a Saturday morning, weather is beautiful and you can just see yourself hiking up that trail with your good mates, having a lough and enjoying the scenery? You reach out to your friends, but unfortunately everyone is busy this weekend. Family, kids, household commitments, etc. Or maybe you moved to a new city, and you just don’t have this kind of a social circle yet, but you don’t want to sacrifice your passions and interests in the name of circumstances?

Well, that’s where Wikendr comes in - a place to organise your weekend and meet new people.

Read More

Wikendr allows you to specify you interests, from Bars and Clubs downtown to cycling, hiking and all sorts of leisure activities. Then, based on your chosen dates and your location, Wikendr will search for matching services in your area, taking into account how much time, you want to devote to your leisure any given day. Let’s say you have a whole day free - just select approximate amount of hours, select what you would like to do and pick a date. It’s easy as that! Wikendr will offer you a selection of services in your local area in a commutable distance based on your requirements and you can book activities there and then as well.

But wait. I can do all this on so many other website, can’t I? And what about my friends? Didn’t I wanted to go and enjoy these activities in a company of good friends?
Yes, Yes and Yes!!! You certainly did! And this is exactly what makes Wikendr so special. After you have indicated what sort of thing you’re into, Wikendr will display you photos and profiles of other people who are interested in the same sort of activities as yourself. Or who have already booked the same place or experience that you are interested in. Or they simply might be searching for something that is very close to what interests you.

Wouldn’t it be nice that you can actually meet a new person, that you otherwise might never have met, who you know for a fact - likes something that you like, just as much as you do? All you have to do is swipe the “right” way and indicate of you are interested in a certain person or not? If you have a “match”, Wikendr will open up a chat window for both of you, so that you can get to know each other, just a little bit better, before meeting up in some special place, that you know for a fact - both of you will enjoy, just as much as the other one.
This is what we mean, by saying “Meet Truly-Like Minded People”.

How Does Wikendr Works?

Become a Member

Getting started couldn’t be any easier, like 1,2,3. Hit the registration button and start planning your Wikendr right away!

First, tell us a little bit about Yourself. We are going to keep all your personal data safe and secure and won’t share with anyone, unless you tell us so.

Read More

So, what actually we need? Just standard stuff, like your date of birth, email, etc. You can upload a picture of yourself, if you like - this can increase your chance of meeting someone special along the way of experiencing all the wonderful and exciting things that you can find in your local area.

In order for us to help you, you will need to Give Us a Clue about what sort of things you are into. Select what types of leisure activities you enjoy from the list provided. You can also provide some additional information about yourself, what things you enjoy in life and anything else you would like other users to know about you.

Thirdly, after registration has been completed, you will be taken to you profile page, where you can use our search engine to start planning your Wikendr right away!

Tell Us About Yourself

So what exactly do we need from you?
Not much, just the standard stuff. Pick your username and password, enter your email, date of birth and select your age. You can also upload your profile picture, which we recommend. This picture will be visible to you and other users who may express their interest to connect with you and start chatting. This will only happen if you and someone who is interested in you will both indicate mutual interest in each other, i.e. “match”.

Give Us a Clue



So what do we mean by this?
All we mean is that we want you to select from the list - what kind of activities are you interested in? It can anything - from bars and restaurants to extreme sports or hiking. Don’t worry too much about what you will select at this stage - you will still be able to search through all of the categories, once you have registered and logged in. We only need this from you at this point in order to pre-select services and offers that will genuinely excite and interest you.
So go on, Give Us A Clue!

Get Your Wikendr Started!

So we assume at this stage, you have already registered and logged in. If not, please follow the steps, described above.
And now you are on your profile page. Here you can enter the dates of your Wikendr, select the activity type that you might be particularly interested in and most importantly select how much time would you like to spend enjoying your perfect Wikendr. You can also type in search terms to help us select the most suitable activities and events for you. Then - hit search!

The results will be generated below and you can view them right away. When you spotted something to your liking, you can just click on this specific item and you will be taken directly to the product/service page, so that you get to know in more detail what is being offered, how far away it is from you, etc.
